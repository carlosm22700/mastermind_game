How to Play Mastermind

  1. Sign Up or Log In: To begin, create an account or sign in to an existing one.
  2. Start a New Game: Once you're on the home page, you can see your last 5 games and start a new game.
  3. Random Combination: Click 'Start Game' to generate a random winning combination and begin the timer.
  4. Make Your Guess: You have 5 minutes to guess a combination of 4 numbers from 0-7. Input your guess in the provided field.
  5. Time Limit: If you don't make a guess within 5 minutes, the session ends and you lose.
  6. Guess Evaluation: After each guess, the win condition is evaluated. You lose if the right combination isn't guessed within 10 attempts or if time runs out.
  7. Winning: Guessing the right combination results is a win!
  8. Quitting a Game: You can quit a game anytime by clicking the 'End Game' button, but this will count as a loss.
  9. Play Again: After each game, you can choose to play again by navigating back to the home page.
